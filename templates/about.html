Technical Overview

The Stochastic Limitation

Traditional GNC systems rely on probabilistic filters (like EKF) to manage sensor noise. These filters perform optimally under Gaussian noise conditions but often fail to detect step-function failures (e.g., sudden solar panel loss) or systematic biases instantly. This latency results in "flying blind"‚Äîrecording corrupted data before the fault is acknowledged.

The Deterministic Solution

MetaSpace introduces a deterministic Fault Detection, Isolation, and Recovery (FDIR) layer. Instead of averaging, it validates sensor data against immutable physical laws (Energy Budget, Orbital Dynamics). If a sensor's output violates a physical invariant, it is immediately isolated from the control loop, ensuring hardware survival and data integrity.
